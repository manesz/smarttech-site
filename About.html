About Smarttech.

         Smart Technology Solution Co., Ltd. aims to be the leader in supplying, setting and providing every kind of network service as well counseling the clients in order that the clients are able to extend their systems and make their systems the most efficient. We are determined to select the leading comprehensive products and solutions and guarantee that the clients investments with us will be worthwhile with leading products, such as Solution.

Factors in Achievement

Experience   We have staff who have got professional experiences in network systems for over 12 years.
Staff   We have a team of engineers who specialize in network system products with certificates, including IBM Certificate, Microsoft Certificate, Linux Redhat Certificate, CCNA, Network Security Certification, VMware Certificate, Power Certificate and many more.
Service   We provide comprehensive services, ranging from providing advice, designing, analyzing, setting and manageing the system as well as after-sales services and standardized outsourced services.
Maintenance   We provide periodic preventive maintenance to test the systems’ status and maintain the equipments so that they are always in their ready states.

Service Principles

                 For over 12 years, Smarttech has had efficient networking and engineering staff who study the clients businesses, determine which solutions or products will fit the clients needs the most efficiently, analyze the worthiness of the project, plan the cooperation with the clients so that the clients will be able to use the solutions and technologies the most efficiently and help the clients expand their business in the future. Smarttech provides various services:

Network System Counseling

                 We offer to analyze network system problems, to provide solutions to the problems, to improve the clients present technologies to the up-to-date technologies and to plan the network system security.

Network System Design Service

                 Considering the clients needs we help guide the clients in order that they are able to use the technologies properly and efficiently and make the systems worth the investment. Also, we help look for the clients opportunity to expand the systems in the future.

Providing Equipments and Setting Network

                 We offer to set up the systems according to the clients needs and enable the clients to use the systems in time within appropriate prices

Out Service

                 We offer to supervise the clients computer systems and network systems. We also manage the systems and solve the clients problems at the clients offices to get the systems to work normally and efficiently.

Staff Training for the Clients
